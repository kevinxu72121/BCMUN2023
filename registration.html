Registration Overview

Overview

British Columbia Model United Nations (BCMUN) is hosted in Vancouver, Canada. This highly anticipated two-day hotel conference provides an opportunity for high school students from around the globe to enhance their public speaking, debate, diplomatic attitudes, and critical thinking skills. BCMUN creates an ideal environment for personal growth and learning, equipping students with the essential skills needed to become effective communicators and future leaders.

BCMUN is an avenue for hundreds of students to expand their knowledge on global issues while forging new friendships. It features a broad range of committees, including UN General Assemblies and advanced crisis committees, where students can participate in discussions and debates on critical issues like climate change, nuclear disarmament, and human rights. The conference's committee simulations offer a unique opportunity for students to gain a deeper understanding of diverse perspectives and cultures. BCMUN is a platform that encourages idea-sharing, cultural exchange, and networking among peers from various parts of the world. Please contact delaffairs@bcmun.org if you have any questions.

Registration Information

Schools should first register their delegation through our delegation registration form, found here. Once a school registers, it will be added to our delegate registration form within 48 hours. Delegates of the school may register once their school is added to the delegate registration form. BCMUN, however, does not require delegates to attend with a delegation. Independent delegates are welcome to stay overnight at our hotel venue.

As part of a TMUN-BCMUN partnership, all delegates who attended TMUN 2023 in person will receive a 15% discount on their delegate registration fee.

Room upgrades will be available closer to the conference. All rooms are quadruple occupancy by default. Prices for delegate registration, before discounts, can be found below:

Refund Policies

Delegates requesting for refunds within one week after their registrations would receive 100% refund.

Delegates requesting for refunds within two week after their registrations would receive 50% refund.

Delegates requesting for refunds three weeks after their registrations would not receive any refund.

*No refund would be issued for any delegate registering after September 30th.
